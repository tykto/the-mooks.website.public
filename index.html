Our Show

Boganocracy

In the second decade of the 21st century, things looked bad... really bad. People battled furiously online, sending angry meme after angry meme. Then, one day, people realised that nothing was getting done and something amazing happened. People started being nice to each other. Previously insurmountable problems were solved and a new age dawned where the world became one.

Except in one place... Australia. In 2117 Australia is an ungoverned wasteland. In constant anarchy, four tribes – the Collingwood Football Club, Retake Australia, Queensland and the Silent Majority – bicker for power. After slaughtering every other sporting team in the country, the Collingwood Football Club still fights for dominance under Captain-Coach-Legend-Stud-Hero Nathan Spacca, Retake Australia, now facing 0% immigration, struggles to find something to hate, while Queensland still tries to scam people by selling time-shares. These three tribes, along with the hauntingly mysterious Silent Majority, battle for supremacy.
